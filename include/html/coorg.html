Coorg - A Haven For Bird Watcher

Madikeri – The Popular Hill Station

If you want to sit back, relax and enjoy a good vacation away from the hustle and bustle of the city, then Madikeri, a small hill station in Karnataka is the place for you.

Coorg or Kodagu promises to greet everyone visiting it with the melodious chirping of some of the most exotic species of birds that one can find in India. The region is one of the prominent tourist destinations in the country, but it’s a little known fact that it is one of the hotspots frequented by bird watchers too.
